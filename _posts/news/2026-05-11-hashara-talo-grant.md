Congratulations to [Hashara Kumarasinghe](/people/kumarasinghe/) for winning the [TALO Computational Biology Innovator Grant](https://jcsmr.anu.edu.au/study/talo-program/talo-innovator-grants)!

Her project, entitled "GPU-Accelerated Maximum Likelihood Phylogenetics in IQ-TREE3: Enabling Large-Scale Evolutionary Analysis on Modern HPC Clusters", has been awarded $10,000 for one year.
